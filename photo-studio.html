How it works

  1. Paste your fal.ai key and your Supabase service_role key (Supabase → Settings → API).
  2. Hit Generate + save all (or per card). Each photo is generated and uploaded to Storage as id.jpg.
  3. It's live in the app immediately — reload Menja and the dish shows its photo. Regenerate any card to redo one.
  4. No Supabase key? Download saves the file locally, or Prompt copies the text for another tool.
One-time setup: in Supabase → Storage, create a public bucket named recipe-photos. That's it — then everything here is one click.
