## After approval — how to use the links
1. In Awin, get your **deep-link / link builder** for the approved advertiser.
2. In the app, the "Buy ingredients" button currently opens a supermarket search
   (`CARREFOUR_SEARCH`). Replace that URL with your **Awin tracking deep link**
   (so clicks are attributed and you earn commission). I can wire this up in
   `app.html` once you have the link.
3. Disclosure: keep the affiliate clause in the Privacy/Terms pages (already added)
   and a small "we may earn a commission" note near the button (good practice + EU law).

## Recommendation
Start with **Carrefour ES + DIA**. Once approved and the tracking link is in the
app, the grocery feature *earns* instead of costs — and we can add the premium
"1-click cart" (Pepesto-style) later, paid for by Premium.
