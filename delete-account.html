Delete your Menja Fresh account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app — no waiting, no forms.

1
Open the Menja Fresh app and make sure you are signed in.
2
Tap the Me tab (person icon) in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete account".
4
Confirm the prompt. Your account and all personal data are permanently deleted immediately — meal plans, preferences, and profile included.

Can't access the app?
Send an email to info@menja-fresh.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and include your registered email address. We will delete your account and confirm within 48 hours.

What gets deleted: your name, email address, meal plans, dietary preferences, liked/disliked recipes, shopping history and all other personal data stored by Menja Fresh. This action is irreversible.
